My first time coding from scratch!

Hello, visitor and welcome to my website! I am Calix Clark, the maker of this, and as stated above, this is my first ever and completely from scratch website! I was able to code this site through Neumont College of Computer Science's 2019 Summer Scholars program. This program was used to teach highschoolers the basics of html in 5 weeks. This course was taught by my father, Timothy Clark, who is currently the VP of acedemic operations at Neumont.Neumont College of Computer Science is a for profit technical college based in Salt Lake City, Utah. They take in students from all over the US and not just Utah. Many of the graduated students have gotten jobs at large tech companies like Amazon and Google. They offer degrees in BSCS (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN COMPUTER SCIENCE), BSGD (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN SOFTWARE AND GAME DEVELOPMENT), BSIS (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN COMPUTER INFORMATION SYSTEMS), BSTM (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT), BSWD (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN WEB DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT) and BSSE (BACHELOR OF SCIENCE IN SOFTWARE ENGINEERING). It was amazing learning where our food came from, and how to raise and cull animals for food.
